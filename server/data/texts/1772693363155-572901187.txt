пГЬ 1.1 E-Commerce (EC) overview 
пГЉ Definition: E-commerce means buying and  selling products or services using the 
Internet or other computer networks. 
пГЉ Purpose: E-commerce helps businesses, sellers, and customers save money, get better 
quality products and services, and get things faster. 
пГЉ Paperless Information Sharing: E-commerce uses different ways to share business 
information without paper, like: 
пВЈ EDI (Electronic Data Exchange) 
пВЈ Email 
пВЈ Online Bulletin Boards 
пВЈ EFT (Electronic Fund Transfer) 
пВЈ Other Network-based Methods

пГЉ EDI (Electronic Data Exchange): A way for businesses to send documents like 
orders or invoices electronically. 
Example: A store sends a purchase order to a supplier using EDI instead of a paper 
form. 
пГЉ Email: Sending and receiving business information through electronic mail. 
Example: A company sends an invoice or update to a customer via email. 
пГЉ Online Bulletin Boards: Websites where businesses post important information 
or updates. 
Example: A company shares news about a new product launch on its online bulletin 
board. 
пГЉ EFT (Electronic Fund Transfer): Transferring money between bank accounts 
electronically. 
Example: A customer pays for an online purchase using a bank transfer or digital 
wallet. 
пГЉ Other Network-based Methods: Using various online tools and technologies to 
share business information. 
Example: Using chat apps or specialized business platforms to send and receive 
documents or updates. 
 
пГЬ 1.2 Features/Aims of E-commerce 
E-Commerce provides following features: 
пГЉ Non-Cash Payment: E-commerce allows payments using credit/debit cards, 
smart cards, and online bank transfers. 
Example: Buying clothes online and paying with a credit card. 
пГЉ 24x7 Service Availability: Businesses can serve customers anytime, any day. 
Example: Shopping online at midnight or on a holiday.

пГЉ Advertising/Marketing: E-commerce helps businesses reach more customers 
and market products better. 
Example: Online ads on social media or websites. 
пГЉ Improved Sales: Orders can be placed anytime without human help, leading to 
more sales. 
Example: Ordering food online at any hour. 
пГЉ Support: E-commerce offers pre-sales and post-sales support for better 
customer service. 
Example: Chat support for questions before buying and help after purchase. 
пГЉ Inventory Management: E-commerce automates product inventory and 
generates reports easily. 
Example: Checking product stock online instantly. 
пГЉ Communication Improvement: E-commerce enables faster and more reliable 
communication     with     customers    and     partners. 
Example: Quick updates via email or messaging apps.

пГЬ Advantages and Limitations of E-Commerce 
пВІ E-commerce benefits to E-Commerce advantages can be broadly classified in three 
major categories: 
o Advantages to Organizations 
o Advantages to Consumers 
o Advantages to Society 
пБґ Benefits of E-Commerce for Organizations: 
пГЉ Global  Reach:  Expands  business  to  national  and  international  markets  with  low 
investment. 
пГЉ Cost Savings: Reduces costs by digitizing information and processes. 
пГЉ Better Service: Allows better interaction and service for more customers. 
пГЉ Improved Supply Chain: Increases productivity and supports on-demand production. 
пГЉ 24/7 Availability: Business can run all day, every day, without extra costs. 
пГЉ Quick  Implementation:  Faster  launch  of  new  ideas  due  to  better  customer 
communication. 
пГЉ Better Inventory Management: Reduces costs related to managing stock. 
пБґ Benefits of E-Commerce for Customers: 
пГЉ 
24x7 Support: Customers can buy products or ask about services anytime and from 
anywhere. 
пГЉ 
More Choices: E-commerce gives more options and faster delivery. 
пГЉ 
Lower Prices: Customers can compare prices to find cheaper deals. 
пГЉ 
Product Reviews: Customers can read reviews from others before buying. 
пГЉ 
Quick Information: Customers can get detailed product info right away. 
пГЉ 
No Sales Tax: In many places, buying online means no sales tax.

пБґ Benefits of E-Commerce for Society: 
пГЉ Telecommuting: People do not need to travel to shop, which reduces traffic and air 
pollution. 
пГЉ Help for the Poor: E-commerce lowers product costs, making it easier for people in 
rural areas to buy things. 
пГЉ Better Living Standards: Online shopping from home can improve people's quality of 
life. 
пГЉ Public Services: E-commerce helps the government provide services like health care, 
education, and social support more efficiently and at lower costs. 
пБґ Disadvantages/Limitations of E-Commerce: 
1. Technical Disadvantages: 
пГЉ Security Issues: E-commerce can have problems with safety and reliability because it 
might not be set up well. 
пГЉ Fast Software Changes: Software keeps changing quickly, which can be hard to keep 
up with. 
пГЉ Limited Internet Speed: In some countries, the internet speed is slow or not strong 
enough. 
2. Non-Technical Disadvantages: 
пГЉ High Initial Cost: Building an e-commerce site can be very expensive and may take 
longer if there are mistakes or lack of experience. 
пГЉ Trust Issues: Customers may not trust unknown sellers and may prefer shopping in 
physical stores. 
пГЉ Product Quality: Online shopping does not let you touch or feel the products before 
buying.

2. Infrastructure: Systems and technology supporting these tools.

1. Public Policy and Privacy Issues: 
пГЉ Sets rules for businesses and customers in e-commerce. 
2. Technical Standards: 
пГЉ Defines formats for information (like multimedia) and network communication 
rules.
